# CampusIQ — User Guide

**Your smart academic companion for university life.**

CampusIQ helps you plan your grades, organise your week, track your study time, and build consistent study habits — all in one place, right on your phone.

---

## What CampusIQ Can Do for You

- Know exactly what scores you need to hit your target CWA
- Keep your class timetable and personal schedule in one view
- Time every study session and see how much you're really putting in
- Build and protect a daily study streak to stay consistent all semester

---

## Getting Around the App

CampusIQ has four main sections. You switch between them using the **bottom navigation bar** at the bottom of your screen.

| Tab | What it does |
|---|---|
| CWA Planner | Plan the grades you need |
| Timetable | Your classes and personal schedule |
| Sessions | Track your study time |
| Streaks | See your consistency over time |

---

## CWA Planner

This tab answers the question: *"What do I need to score to hit my target CWA?"*

### Adding your courses

1. Tap the **+ button** (floating button at the bottom right).
2. A form slides up — fill in:
   - Course code (e.g. MATH 151)
   - Course name
   - Credit hours
   - The score you expect or are aiming for
3. Tap **Save**. The course appears on your list instantly.

### Seeing your projected CWA

As soon as you add courses, a **summary bar** at the top shows:
- Your **projected CWA** — based on the scores you entered
- Your **target CWA** — the goal you set for yourself
- The **gap** — how far you are from your target

Everything updates the moment you change a score — no need to save manually.

### Adjusting expected scores

Each course card has a **slider** you can drag left or right to raise or lower your expected score. Your projected CWA recalculates in real time as you drag.

### Setting your target CWA

Tap the target indicator on the summary bar to open a dialog where you type in your personal CWA goal (e.g. 3.5). The gap indicator will update to show whether you are above or below that target.

### The high-impact badge

One course will be marked with a badge — this is the course carrying the most credit hours. Improving your score in this course has the biggest effect on your overall CWA.

### Editing or deleting a course

Tap any course card to open the edit form. To delete, use the delete option inside that form.

## Timetable

This tab holds two layers in one screen: your **official class timetable** and your **personal schedule**. You switch between the layers by swiping left or right.

### The three views

Swipe left or right to move between:

- **Class Only** — shows only your university class slots
- **Both** (default) — shows classes and personal slots together
- **Personal Only** — shows only your personal schedule

A small page indicator at the top shows which view you are on.

### Switching days

Tap any day name at the top (Mon, Tue, Wed…) or swipe the day selector to jump to a different day. The grid covers Monday to Saturday, from 6AM to 8PM.

---

### Adding a class slot

1. Tap the **+ button** or tap an empty cell in the time grid.
2. Fill in:
   - Course code and name
   - Venue (e.g. Great Hall, Room 14)
   - Type: Lecture, Practical, or Tutorial
   - Start and end time
   - A colour to make it easy to spot
3. Tap **Save**. The slot appears in the grid immediately.

Tap any class slot to see its details or delete it.

### Free time blocks

When you have a gap between classes, the app automatically detects and displays it as a **free block** in the grid. This helps you spot where you have time to study or take a break.

---

### Adding a personal slot

Personal slots are things you schedule yourself — study sessions, gym, meals, rest, and more.

1. Switch to **Personal Only** or **Both** view.
2. Tap the **+ button**.
3. Fill in:
   - Category — choose from: Study, Gym, Rest, Meal, Side Project, Devotion, Errand, or Custom
   - Start and end time
   - How often it repeats:
     - **One-off** — just this specific day
     - **Daily** — shows up every day
     - **Weekly** — shows up on the same day every week
   - A colour of your choice
4. Tap **Save**.

Recurring slots are added once and appear automatically on future days — you never have to re-enter them.

Tap any personal slot to view its details or delete it.

## Sessions

This tab is where you time your study sessions and see how much work you are putting in.

### Starting a study session

1. Tap the **+ button** or the start prompt on the Sessions screen.
2. A **course picker** slides up, showing your CWA courses and today's timetable slots.
3. Tap the course you are about to study.
4. The timer starts immediately.

### While a session is running

- A **timer card** at the top of the Sessions screen shows the course and elapsed time.
- If you switch to another tab, a small **floating mini-timer** appears at the bottom of the screen so you always know a session is running. Tap it to jump back to the Sessions tab.

### Stopping a session

Tap the **Stop** button on the timer card. The session is saved automatically with the course name, date, and total duration.

### Your session history

Below the timer, a scrollable list shows all your past study sessions — the course, date, and how long you studied. Most recent sessions appear at the top.

### Analytics dashboard

Scroll down on the Sessions screen to see:

- **Daily total** — how many hours you studied today
- **Weekly bar chart** — a bar for each day of the week showing your study time at a glance
- **Per-course breakdown** — how your total study time is split across your courses
- **Planned vs actual** — compares the time you have scheduled in your timetable against the time you actually studied

## Streaks

This tab keeps you accountable by tracking how consistent you are.

### Your study streak

Your **study streak** counts how many days in a row you have completed at least one study session. The streak hero card at the top shows:
- Your **current streak** — days in a row right now
- Your **longest streak** — your all-time best

Your streak stays alive if you studied yesterday but have not studied yet today — it only breaks if a full day passes with no sessions.

### Per-course streaks

Below the main streak, you can see individual streaks for each of your courses — so you can spot which subjects you have been consistent with and which ones need more attention.

### Attendance tracker

Mark the days you actually attended class. The app uses this to calculate your **attendance streak** — how many consecutive days you showed up.

### Milestones

CampusIQ celebrates your consistency with milestones at:
**3 · 7 · 14 · 21 · 30 · 40 · 50 · 60 · 70 · 80 · 90 · 100 days**

The **milestone grid** shows all milestones as cards. Reached milestones are unlocked and highlighted. Locked ones show what is coming next.

The **next milestone card** tells you exactly how many more days you need to unlock your next achievement.

### Activity heatmap

A calendar-style heatmap shows your study activity across recent weeks. Darker squares mean more time studied on that day. It gives you a visual snapshot of your semester at a glance.

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of CampusIQ

- **Set up your class timetable first** — the session tracker and free time detector rely on it.
- **Add all your courses to the CWA planner** before the semester gets busy — adjust scores as results come in.
- **Start a session every time you sit down to study**, even for short periods. The weekly chart and per-course breakdown only become useful with consistent logging.
- **Check your streak daily** — knowing your streak is on the line is one of the best motivators to open your books.
- **Use personal slots for recurring habits** (e.g. "Study — daily 8PM–10PM") so they show up automatically every day without re-adding them.

---

## Everything is Saved Automatically

CampusIQ stores all your data directly on your phone. There is no account to create and no internet connection needed. Your courses, timetable, sessions, and streaks are all there the next time you open the app.
